About Me

Who I am, what drives me, and where I'm headed.

I am a Secondary 4 student at CHIJ Secondary (Toa Payoh), driven by a deep belief that leadership is most powerful when it serves others. Over four years, I have sought roles that push me to grow — from organising large-scaled events as CCA Vice-President, to representing my school at international debate championship.

"Leadership is not about being in charge — it is about taking care of those in your charge."

My journey in debate taught me to think critically under pressure, embrace multiple perspectives, and communicate with precision and conviction. These skills shape how I lead, how I listen, and how I approach every challenge.

Beyond school, I am committed to community service — working at Vietnamese Youth Alliance in Singapore (VNYA) for community welfare at and founding a youth initiative Learn2earn that has reached over 1030 beneficiaries. For me, leadership without service is incomplete.

In the humanities, I have developed genuine curiosity about geography, current affairs, and current policy — interests that ground how I engage with the world and with people around me.

I am applying to JC via DSA under Community Leadership and Debate, seeking an environment that challenges me to think bigger and contribute more meaningfully to society.

Community service shaped my leadership philosophy: before making decisions for others, you must first understand their lives. I carry that into every role I take on.
